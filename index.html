ABOUT THE CHANNEL

BlockMachine started as a single survival world and turned into a full-time obsession. Every episode is one more attempt to build something worth showing — and usually one more creeper explosion I didn't see coming.

No scripts, no perfect runs. Just real Minecraft, recorded as it happens, uploaded for anyone who'd rather watch someone else lose their inventory in lava.
